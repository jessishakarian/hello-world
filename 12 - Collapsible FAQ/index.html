Frequently Asked Questions About The Imperial TIE Fighter

Why is the TIE Fighter called that?

They have Twin Ion Engines.

What is the top speed on the imperial TIE Fighters?

1,200km/hr or 745mph with both engines at full power.

What's unique about the TIE Fighter?

There are no moving parts on the ship, so the engine doesn't really break down. However, it means they're more likely to be shot down rather than break down. The TIE Fighter also has a propulsion system that makes them fast and efficient ships compared to most commercial ships.

Why does the engine make that roaring sound?

The engines naturally make that noise.

How does it differ from a x-wing?

There are no hyperdrive, no need for an astromech, and has no built-in life support system (due to the pressurized pilot suits).
